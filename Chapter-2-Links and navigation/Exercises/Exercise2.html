Overview

The Fast and the Furious is a franchise centered around a series of action films that are largely concerned with illegal street racing, heists, and espionage.

Key Information: The franchise started in 2001 with its first film, The Fast and the Furious, and has since spawned numerous sequels, spin-offs, and short films.

Cast

The franchise has featured a variety of actors throughout its films, including Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Dwayne Johnson, Michelle Rodriguez, and many more.

Reviews

Reviews for the Fast and Furious franchise have been generally positive, with praise for its action sequences, cast performances, and entertainment value.
